Feature: Email Classification and Prioritization
Description: The Email Router system will automatically categorize and prioritize incoming emails based on content analysis and urgency to streamline the workflow for Customer Support Representatives.
Key Functionality:
- Utilize LLM-based classifiers to analyze email content and determine intent and category.
- Assign confidence scores to prioritize emails for efficient handling.
User Benefit: Customer Support Representatives can focus on addressing complex customer inquiries effectively as routine emails are automatically sorted and prioritized.

Feature: Performance Monitoring Dashboard
Description: The Email Router system will provide IT Administrators with a comprehensive dashboard to monitor system performance metrics, ensuring optimal operation and facilitating necessary adjustments.
Key Functionality:
- Display real-time performance metrics such as response times and accuracy.
- Offer configuration options for system settings and adjustments.
User Benefit: IT Administrators can proactively manage and optimize the Email Router system to maintain efficient email processing capabilities.

Feature: Intelligent Inquiry Routing
Description: The Email Router system will intelligently route relevant and complex inquiries to Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) based on content analysis and predefined rules, ensuring specialized expertise is directed where needed.
Key Functionality:
- Develop rules-based engine to assign emails to appropriate SMEs.
- Include relevant context and metadata when forwarding emails to SMEs.
User Benefit: SMEs receive only inquiries that require their specialized expertise, improving efficiency and job satisfaction by reducing routine inquiries.

Feature: Automated Response Generation
Description: The Email Router system will automate responses to routine inquiries using a RAG system, freeing up time for Team Leaders and their teams to focus on higher-value activities and increasing overall productivity.
Key Functionality:
- Deploy RAG system to generate contextually accurate responses.
- Incorporate an approval workflow for reviewing and editing automated responses.
User Benefit: Team Leaders can redirect their team's efforts towards tasks that require human expertise, enhancing productivity and job satisfaction.

Feature: Compliance Assurance
Description: The Email Router system will ensure that all communications adhere to organizational standards and regulatory requirements, reducing the risk of non-compliance and potential legal issues for Compliance Officers.
Key Functionality:
- Implement data privacy and compliance measures such as encryption and access control.
- Anonymize or mask Personally Identifiable Information (PII) when required.
User Benefit: Compliance Officers can rely on the Email Router system to maintain compliance with regulations, mitigating risks and ensuring legal adherence in communication processes.
